EduWell Campus Stablished Since 1980

eduwell is the trusted platform for education and learning. Founded by Harvard and MIT, eduwell is home to more than 20 million learners, the majority of top-ranked universities in the world and industry-leading companies. As a global nonprofit, eduwell is transforming traditional education, removing the barriers of cost, location and access. Fulfilling the demand for people to learn on their own terms, eduwell is reimagining the possibilities of education, providing the highest-quality, stackable learning experiences including the groundbreaking MicroMasters® programs. Supporting learners at every stage, whether entering the job market, changing fields, seeking a promotion or exploring new interests, eduwell delivers courses for curious minds on topics ranging from data and computer science to leadership and communications. eduwell is where you go to learn.
